The Last Ember Throne

A dying kingdom. A stolen crown. One ember that refuses to go out.

When the capital falls in a single night of fire, the last heir to the Ember Throne is left with nothing but a cracked signet ring and a debt to the dead. To take back what was burned away, she'll have to bargain with the very thing that destroyed her home — and figure out how much of herself she's willing to trade for a crown that might not want her back.
